Our Story

Supporting Filipino Artisans Since 2010

Habi Pinoy Crafts was founded to connect local weavers, carvers, and artisans from across the Philippines to global markets. We ensure fair wages and preserve age-old crafting techniques passed down through generations.

Every purchase helps sustain families and keep Filipino cultural heritage alive.
